## 6. Summary & Key Insights

The critical stakeholders for project success are the Hospital Manager,
Doctors, IT Department, and Patients. The Hospital Manager is responsible for
defining scheduling requirements and ensuring all shifts are properly staffed.
Doctors are the primary users of the system and rely on it to create fair
schedules that accommodate preferences, vacations, and workload distribution.
The IT Department plays a key role in hosting, monitoring, and maintaining the
scheduling system to ensure reliability and security. Patients are indirectly
affected by the project because proper staffing helps ensure timely and
effective emergency care. The main concerns include balancing doctor
preferences with staffing needs, ensuring the system functions reliably, and
maintaining continuous coverage for patient care. To address these concerns,
the project team should maintain regular communication with the Hospital
Manager, involve doctors in testing and feedback sessions, collaborate closely
with the IT Department throughout development, and prioritize features that
support efficient scheduling and quality patient care.
