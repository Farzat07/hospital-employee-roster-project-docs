## 1. Project Purpose / Business Need

The employee roster (shift schedule) at the hospital's emergency department is
being generated manually, taking time and effort. This leads to less release
lead-time before the month starts, and makes modifications and customizations
difficult and ad-hoc.

The solution benefits several stakeholder groups, including managers, doctors,
and patients:

- By automating employee rostering, schedules can be generated ahead of time,
  giving doctors better predictability and room for modifications if necessary.
- Shift swaps can be integrated into the system, giving managers better
  visibility when they happen.
- Shifts can be distributed more fairly, protecting doctors and reducing the
  hospital's legal risks.
- Doctors can see personalized calendars showing only their shifts, improving
  usability.
- It can improve doctors' experience by allowing them to set preferences that
  fit their lifestyles, enabling better work-life balance.
